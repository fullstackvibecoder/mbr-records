## What this series is

A continuing satirical bit in which George Brett Olson, speaking as the **Caucasian Caucus**, conducts UN-style negotiations with other "delegations" of American culture. Decisions are framed as treaties, accords, vetoes, and rulings, ratified by various councils. The recurring gathering is the **MF Function** (originally the "Motherfucking Function," renamed because the Bay Area Black Delegation rejected "frickin'").
