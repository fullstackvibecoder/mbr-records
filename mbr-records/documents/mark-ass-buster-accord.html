The Mark Ass Buster Accord

Returning "Mark Ass Buster" (and permissible variants) to circulation in exchange for the Caucasian Caucus's restoration of Numb Nuts, supervised Skating on Thin Ice, and Knuckle Sandwich.

Whereas the Caucasian Caucus did, on or about March 8, 2026, open negotiations for the return of certain phrases by offering "Scallywag" and "Rapscallion", and the offer of "Scallywag" was rejected on March 9 on the precedent of Project Pat's "Chicken Head";

Whereas the parties exchanged competing terms over multiple proceedings, including a brief and ill-considered offer of raisin reintroduction (since superseded by the permanent Arab veto);

Whereas on March 21 the Caucasian Caucus did, in formal apology, withdraw the proposed phrase "Ouch Bumps and Thunder Cookies" and substitute "Knuckle Sandwich";

Now, therefore, the parties enter into the following Mark Ass Buster Accord:

Article I — Restoration by the Black Delegation

The Black Delegation hereby returns to general circulation the phrase "Mark Ass Buster", together with the permissible variants "Mark ass bluster" and "Buster ass Mark", all of which shall be of equal standing.

Article II — Restoration by the Caucasian Caucus

In consideration, the Caucasian Caucus returns to general circulation the following:

  1. Numb Nuts — under full provisional terms;
  2. Skating on Thin Ice — subject to Article III below;
  3. Knuckle Sandwich — substituted on March 21 for the withdrawn proposal "Ouch Bumps and Thunder Cookies".

Article III — Use Condition for "Skating on Thin Ice"

The phrase "Skating on Thin Ice" may be employed by any delegate only when followed, in the same utterance, by one of the following terms of address:

  1. "buddy";
  2. "pal"; or
  3. "bucko".

Use of the phrase without one of the above shall be void ab initio and may be entered into the record as a procedural lapse.

Article IV — Withdrawn Proposals

The following proposals are formally withdrawn and shall have no effect under this accord:

  1. Scallywag — rejected March 9 (Black property; precedent: Project Pat, "Chicken Head");
  2. Rapscallion — neither accepted nor rejected; remains on the docket as a proposed item;
  3. Ouch Bumps and Thunder Cookies — retracted March 21 with apology, replaced by Knuckle Sandwich.
Note of Record. The Caucasian Caucus's apology of March 21 was tendered in the following terms: "the Caucasian caucus would like to apologize on my behalf for trying to make ouch bumps and thunder cookies happen."
