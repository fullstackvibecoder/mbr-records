Whereas the inventory of "Holy ___" phrases of the chamber requires comprehensive disposition;

Whereas the On My Mama Accords (MBR-ACC-2026-016) settled certain custody questions in favor of the League of Latinos, but did not exhaust the inventory;

Whereas the Italian Consigliere did, during private sit-downs, offer Holy Guacamole in a manner that conflicts with the Latino claim of April 17;

Now, therefore, the floor is opened on April 30, 2026, with the following interim dispositions and one re-opened question:

Article I — Holy Macaroni

Holy Macaroni is acknowledged as Italian property, with joint custody granted to the Black Delegation for the limited purpose of mac and cheese.

Joint custody under this Article does not extend to spaghetti, ziti, or any short pasta unrelated to mac and cheese.

Article II — Holy Smokes

Holy Smokes is acknowledged as Jamaican intellectual property, characterized as a Rasta product. The phrase is licensed to the Caucasian Delegation on standard licensing terms, the particulars of which shall be set forth in a separate license agreement filed by the Bureau.

Article III — Holy Guacamole (RE-OPENED)

The ownership of Holy Guacamole, previously conceded to the League of Latinos under the On My Mama Accords (Article IV), is hereby RE-OPENED to the floor in light of the Italian Consigliere's prior offer.

Pending resolution:

  1. The Latino claim of April 17 remains on file;
  2. The Italian offer made during sit-downs is acknowledged as a competing claim;
  3. No party shall act in reliance upon any provisional custody until the floor disposes of the matter.
Status Note. Articles I and II are filed in DRAFT pending formal floor concurrence. Article III is held entirely OPEN. The Bureau shall update this filing upon ratification.
