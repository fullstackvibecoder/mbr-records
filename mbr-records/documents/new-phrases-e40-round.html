Whereas the Black Betterment Consortium, in performance of its undertaking under Article IV of the E-40 Accords, did produce a roster of new phrases for selective ratification by the Council;

Whereas the Council has, after due deliberation, accepted certain phrases and rejected others;

Now, therefore, the following dispositions are entered into the record:

Article I — Phrases Ratified

The following phrases are RATIFIED and shall circulate without restriction:

  1. "You're in a china shop, buddy, and you're about to get the bull" — full sentence; substitution permitted only at the bull/buddy slots.
  2. "Shooting that across their bow" — adverbial; for use in advance warning.
  3. "Mayonated" — the Caucasian counterpart to "melanated", ratified April 25.
  4. "Cheetah Fin" — noun; usage to be determined by the floor.

Article II — Phrase Not Ratified

The phrase "Dip Quad" is, by Council resolution of April 25, 2026, NOT RATIFIED. The phrase is final per Council resolution and shall not be reintroduced under this filing.

Article III — Alternatives Held in Committee

The following alternatives to the rejected proposal remain under committee review:

  1. "Dip Dattle";
  2. "Fiddly Quad".

Each shall, if ratified, be filed by addendum to this accord. Neither shall be deemed in circulation until so filed.

Procedural Note. The Council reserves selective ratification authority over all subsequent productions of the Black Betterment Consortium under the E-40 Accords. Bulk ratification is not contemplated.
