The Naming Accord of the MF Function

Settling the official name of the standing assembly through a three-option floor vote and a subsequent rename forced by the Bay Area Black Delegation.

Whereas the standing gathering of delegations had, prior to March 14, operated without a settled name, leading to procedural confusion in the record;

Whereas the chair did open the floor to three nominated options on March 14, with the Cornflake Raisin Contingency Plan held in reserve as escalation;

Whereas the floor vote produced a clear winner, but the resulting name was found insufficiently emphatic by the Bay Area Black Delegation;

Now, therefore, the following Naming Accord is hereby enacted:

Article I — The Floor Vote of March 14

The floor was offered three (3) options, all duly seconded:

  1. The Shindig;
  2. The Hoedown;
  3. The Motherfricking Function ("MFF").

The MFF carried the vote. Shindig and Hoedown are accordingly retired as nominees and shall not be re-introduced under this accord.

Article II — Rename Forced by the Bay Area Black Delegation

On March 16, 2026, the Bay Area Black Delegation, exercising its standing authority over matters of vernacular emphasis, formally rejected the use of "frickin'" as insufficiently emphatic, and required substitution. The matter being one of cultural authority, the floor concurred without further vote.

The official name of the standing assembly is, accordingly, the MF Function.

Article III — Permitted Forms

The following forms of the name are equally proper and may be used interchangeably in the record, in correspondence, and in proceedings:

  1. MF Function;
  2. MFF;
  3. The Function (informal).

The unredacted form is not entered into the public record; it is preserved unspoken in the spaces between letters.

Procedural Note. The Cornflake Raisin Contingency Plan, held in reserve during this naming, was not invoked. It remains a standing escalation tool of the chamber (see MBR-DEC-2026-004).
