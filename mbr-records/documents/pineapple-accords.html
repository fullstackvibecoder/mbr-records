The Pineapple Accords

An accord between the Gabagoo Guild (Italian Delegation) and the cross-delegation Pineapple Posse, settling matters of pizza, pasta, and pineapple jurisdiction.

Whereas the standing of pineapple as a permitted topping upon pizza has long divided the chamber, and the Italian Delegation, on or about March 24, did formally constitute itself as the Gabagoo Guild for the express purpose of regulating same;

Whereas a cross-delegation body, styling itself the Pineapple Posse, has asserted a competing claim of fusion practice;

Whereas the breaking of pasta has, over many proceedings, been identified as an ongoing offense to the Gabagoo Guild and to good order generally;

Whereas the parties, in good faith and with mutual concession, have produced the following terms;

Now, therefore, let it be enacted on this twenty-fifth day of March, two thousand twenty-six, the following Pineapple Accords:

Article I — Concessions of the Gabagoo Guild

The Gabagoo Guild, on behalf of the Italian Delegation, grants the following to all delegates of the chamber:

  1. Mamma Mia. Full and unrestricted use of the phrase "Mamma Mia" is hereby extended to all delegates, irrespective of delegation;
  2. Espresso. Espresso shall be supplied on a continuing basis to the standing function, scheduled and uninterrupted;
  3. Cannolis. Nana's homemade cannolis shall arrive on schedule and in adequate quantity, in perpetuity, or until withdrawn by Nana herself.

Article II — Cessation of the Breaking of Pasta

Effective immediately upon ratification of this accord, all breaking of pasta at the MF Function shall cease. No delegate, guest, observer, or sub-caucus shall break pasta within the proceedings under any pretext.

The Gabagoo Guild's enforcement committee shall report violations directly to the floor; the Council of Elder Aunties shall hold concurrent jurisdiction.

Article III — Restrictions Upon the Pineapple Posse

The Pineapple Posse, in exchange for recognition of its fusion practice, accepts the following restrictions:

  1. Baked-in only. All pineapple, where placed upon pizza, must be baked in — no pineapple shall be applied "on the side", as topping after the fact, or as garnish;
  2. Separate table. The Pineapple Posse's fusion-pizza table shall be seated out of sight from the Gabagoo Guild's main table at every standing function;
  3. Reserved veto of the Guild. The Gabagoo Guild retains full topping veto at its own table; this veto does not extend to the fusion table, and the fusion table's veto does not extend to the Guild table.

Article IV — Hawaiian Acknowledgment

The phrase "at this time" is acknowledged in this accord as the property of the Hawaiian Delegation, and is reserved unto them. No party to this accord asserts a competing claim.

Article V — Address of the House of Habibi

By concurrent council vote at the same sitting, the address known as the House of Habibi is ratified as the standing meeting place of the Coalition of Habibis. The matter is incorporated into this filing for completeness of the record.

Procurement Note. Espresso and Nana's homemade cannolis continue to arrive at MF Function on schedule under the terms of Article I. Markets stable. No price action.
