The Snickerdoodle Recompense

Following the failed Banana Pudding offer of March 30, the Caucasian Caucus releases Gamm Gamm's snickerdoodle recipe, free of charge, as recompense for skating on thin ice with the chamber.

Whereas on March 30, 2026, the Caucasian Caucus, through the chair, did offer an overnight oats recipe in exchange for the Auntie Army's banana pudding recipe;

Whereas the offer was, on the same day, hacked into the feed and rejected categorically by Cookie Williams of the Auntie Army;

Whereas the chair was thereafter found to be skating on thin ice with the chamber, dangerously close to having the white card revoked;

Now, therefore, the Caucasian Caucus, in lieu of further sanction and as a unilateral act of recompense, releases the following:

Article I — Release

The Caucasian Caucus hereby releases, into the public domain and free of charge, the recipe for Gamm Gamm's snickerdoodles. The release is:

  1. Unconditional — no exchange is asked, none shall be tendered;
  2. Permanent — the recipe shall not be retracted or revoked;
  3. Effective immediately upon filing.

Article II — Effect on the Banana Pudding Matter

For the avoidance of doubt: this release shall not be construed as opening, reopening, or otherwise altering the Caucasian Caucus's standing on the matter of banana pudding. The Auntie Army's recipe remains, as ever, the property of the Auntie Army; no further offer is contemplated.

Article III — Status of the Chair

The chair acknowledges:

  1. That the offer of March 30 was made in poor judgment;
  2. That the white card remains in force, but on conditional standing;
  3. That further misjudgments may result in formal sanction.
Note of Record. The release of Gamm Gamm's snickerdoodle recipe is entered free of charge. No reciprocal obligation arises in any party.
