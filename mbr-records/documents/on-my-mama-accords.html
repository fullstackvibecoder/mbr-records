Whereas the phrase "On My Mama" is, and shall remain, the property of the Black Delegation;

Whereas the Caucasian Caucus has, on numerous occasions, expressed a desire for limited and supervised use of said phrase;

Whereas the Black Delegation, on April 17, set out preliminary terms (Holy Moly, Holy Guacamole, Heavens to Betsies, and the cessation of cursing at moms in public, with final ruling reserved to Kevin Durant);

Whereas the League of Latinos asserted its long-standing claim to Holy Guacamole and Holy Frijoles, which claims have been honored;

Whereas on April 25 the Caucasian Caucus accepted the final stipulations and threw in "Cutie Patootie" free of charge;

Whereas on April 27 the Atlanta Delegation introduced the F Around and Find Out (FAFO) Clause, ratified by the Council of Elder Aunties as addendum to this accord;

Now, therefore, the parties enter into the following On My Mama Accords:

Article I — Limitation of Use

The Caucasian Caucus may use the phrase "On My Mama" only once per calendar year. No more, no less. The annual reset shall align with the calendar of the Council of Elder Aunties.

Article II — Witness and Notarization

The first (and only annual) use of "On My Mama" by the Caucasian Caucus must be:

  1. Witnessed in real time by a member of the Black Delegation;
  2. Notarized by said witness, who shall enter the use into the Bureau's record by the next standing day.

An unwitnessed use is, by operation of this Article, void ab initio — and shall be treated, for the purposes of penalty, as a violation under Article III.

Article III — Penalty for Unseasoned Use

If the use is, in the judgment of the witness or any senior delegate of the Black Delegation, deemed unseasoned in delivery, in use, or in context, the offending member of the Caucasian Caucus shall be subject to a lifetime ban from any further use of the phrase, with no possibility of appeal.

"Unseasoned" shall be construed broadly. Doubt resolves in favor of the Black Delegation.

Article IV — Concessions to the League of Latinos

In consideration of the foregoing, and in resolution of the parallel claim raised on April 17, the Caucasian Caucus formally concedes to the League of Latinos, in perpetuity:

  1. Holy Guacamole;
  2. Holy Frijoles;
  3. Holy Toledo (recognized April 20);
  4. Holy Mole (recognized April 20).

Article V — Free-of-Charge Inclusion

The Caucasian Caucus, in good faith and as token of ratification, hereby throws in "Cutie Patootie" free of charge, with no exchange asked. The phrase shall thereafter circulate without restriction.

Addendum I — F Around and Find Out (FAFO) Clause

Introduced by the Atlanta Delegation; ratified by the Council of Elder Aunties on April 27, 2026.

Where any member of the Caucasian Caucus invokes the phrase "On My Mama" in violation of Articles I, II, or III, the consequences may include, but are not limited to, the following — each cumulative, none in limitation of any other:

  1. The offender shall be spiritually escorted back to their lane;
  2. The offender shall be folded like hot laundry;
  3. The offender shall receive a mouth check (two-piece), immediately followed by the hymn "Try Jesus, Don't Try Me".

The above are illustrative, not exhaustive. The Auntie Army holds enforcement; the Council of Elder Aunties holds final word.
