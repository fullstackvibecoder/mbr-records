Decree on Sunday Closure

Standing decree of the Council of Elder Aunties: the chamber does not convene on Sundays.

Whereas the chamber requires a settled calendar of operation;

Whereas the Council of Elder Aunties has, in plenary session, considered the question of weekly schedule;

Now, therefore, the Council decrees:

The Decree

The MF Function shall not convene on Sundays. The reason of record is plain: it's church.

  1. No proceedings, votes, ratifications, or filings shall be entered into the record on a Sunday;
  2. The Bureau shall not certify any document dated on a Sunday;
  3. Saturday business properly carried over shall resume on the following Monday, with the Recap on Mondays standing as the formal entry point of the new cycle.
Liturgical Note. The closing benediction of all proceedings — "God is good all the time, and all the time, God is good" — applies on every day on which the chamber convenes, but most especially on the day before Sunday and the day after.
