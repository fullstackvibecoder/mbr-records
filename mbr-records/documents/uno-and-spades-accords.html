The UNO & Spades Accords

Whereas the chamber, having matured to the point of game-time governance, did open formal proceedings on the regulation of UNO and Spades at all standing functions;

Whereas Spades has, by long tradition, been recognized as a house-rules-driven game of "Olympic gymnastics" difficulty, properly governed only by those who have earned the right;

Whereas the Black Delegation has, in good faith, opened a tri-delegation exploratory committee on UNO rules;

Now, therefore, the following UNO & Spades Accords are enacted on this twentieth day of March, two thousand twenty-six:

Article I — UNO Rules Exploratory Committee

A tri-delegation exploratory committee is hereby chartered, comprising:

  1. The Black Delegation (convening);
  2. The Arab Delegation;
  3. The Asian Association.

The committee shall report to the floor on a schedule of its own determination. Until such report, UNO at the MF Function shall be played under "house rules of the host", with disputes referred to the Council of Elder Aunties.

Article II — Lease of "Cool Your Jets Bro"

The phrase "Cool Your Jets Bro", the property of the Caucasian Caucus, is hereby leased to the Black Delegation for use during all Spades and UNO proceedings. The lease is:

  1. Time-unbounded but revocable on thirty (30) days' notice;
  2. Conditional upon non-derogatory use within the spirit of game-time mediation.

Article III — Contribution by the Latino League

The League of Latinos contributes the address "Tranquilo Mommy/Poppy" to the chamber's pool of game-time mediations. The contribution is unconditional and shall remain in good standing in perpetuity.

Article IV — Spades: Recognition and Restraint

The chamber formally acknowledges Spades as a game of "Olympic gymnastics" difficulty, the rules of which are not subject to floor amendment. Governance is reserved to the Black Delegation in plenary.

Order of the Chair. George Brett Olson, by ruling of the floor on March 19, is hereby ordered to stop talking about Spades. The order is of indefinite duration; appeal lies only to the Council of Elder Aunties.
