The E-40 Accords

Granting a two-minute consultation with E-40 in exchange for a five-part bundle from the Caucasian Caucus, with custodial terms regarding Sir Ian McKellen and Shadowfax.

Whereas the Caucasian Caucus has long sought audience with E-40 for the purpose of receiving instruction in the production of new phrases;

Whereas the Yay Area Black Delegation holds the keys to said audience and is the only body authorized to grant it;

Whereas the parties have, after extended exchange, settled on a fair and proportionate consideration;

Now, therefore, the parties enter into the following E-40 Accords, on this thirteenth day of April, two thousand twenty-six:

Article I — Grant by the Yay Area Black Delegation

The Yay Area Black Delegation grants the Caucasian Caucus a single, scheduled, in-person two-minute (2:00) consultation with E-40, the subject of which shall be the coining of new white-people phrases. The consultation shall be:

  1. Strictly time-limited to two minutes, exclusive of pleasantries;
  2. Recorded by the Bureau for the record;
  3. Non-transferable, non-renewable, and non-extendable.

Article II — Consideration from the Caucasian Caucus

In exchange for the Article I grant, the Caucasian Caucus shall convey, in a single bundle, the following five parts:

  1. Lease of Gandalf the White (to wit, Sir Ian McKellen) for a period of one (1) calendar month, with full residence privileges at the Yay Area Black Delegation;
  2. Custodial transfer of Shadowfax, with full petting and riding rights, included in the Article I lease at no additional charge;
  3. One audiobook reading by Sir Patrick Stewart, of any title nominated by the Yay Area Black Delegation;
  4. Free bowling lessons from The Big Lebowski, scheduled at the convenience of the Yay Area Black Delegation;
  5. The undertaking by the Black Betterment Consortium to subsequently produce a roster of new Black-people phrases, ratified or not at the Council's discretion.

Article III — Custody of Sir Ian McKellen and Shadowfax

For the duration of the Article II lease:

  1. Sir Ian McKellen shall reside in good order with the Yay Area Black Delegation, with all reasonable comforts;
  2. Shadowfax shall be quartered as Sir Ian directs, with daily exercise privileges;
  3. Petting privileges in respect of Shadowfax extend to all delegates of the Yay Area Black Delegation and their immediate family;
  4. Riding privileges are exercised at the rider's own risk and shall not be construed to include speed exceeding that proper to a horse.
Roster Note. Sir Ian McKellen, lent under these Accords, remains in residence with the Yay Area Black Delegation. Shadowfax accommodations are confirmed; petting privileges in effect.

Article IV — Forward Production

Upon completion of the Article I consultation, the Black Betterment Consortium shall convene to produce, in good faith, a roster of new Black-people phrases. Said phrases shall be:

  1. Submitted to the Council of Elder Aunties for selective ratification;
  2. Filed by the Bureau under a separate proceeding (the "E-40 Round"), as and when ratified;
  3. Subject to amendment, withdrawal, or further consideration by the Yay Area Black Delegation.
