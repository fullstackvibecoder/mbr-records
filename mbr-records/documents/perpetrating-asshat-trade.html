Whereas the Black Delegation has, in good faith, offered to return the verb "Perpetrating" and its derivatives to general circulation;

Whereas the LBC Spicy Mayo Sapiens of the Caucasian Caucus did, on March 28, throw in "Assclown" as a token of good faith in addition to the principal terms;

Whereas the West Coast Black Delegation has additionally offered "fronting", conditional upon the Atlanta response on "capping";

Now, therefore, the following terms are hereby ratified by the Council of Elder Aunties:

Article I — Restoration by the Black Delegation

The Black Delegation returns to circulation:

  1. Perpetrating, in its full form;
  2. The shortened forms "purporting", "purpin'", and "perp", all of equal standing.

The Council ratifies the terms in plenary: "The Elder Auntie Council has ratified the word perpetrating. Also a shortened version of either purporting, purpin, or perp."

Article II — Restoration by the Caucasian Caucus

In consideration, the Caucasian Caucus returns to circulation:

  1. Asshat — principal term;
  2. Assclown — added by the LBC Spicy Mayo Sapiens as a token of good faith;
  3. Tomfoolery — in full effect, for the avoidance of all related foolery.

Article III — Conditional Inclusion: "Fronting"

The West Coast Black Delegation has offered the inclusion of "fronting" as a further return. This inclusion is held in abeyance until the Atlanta Black Delegation files its formal position on the proposed retraction of "capping". Should Atlanta concur, "fronting" shall be added to Article I by addendum without further ratification.

Article IV — Liaisons re: Spicy/Seasoned Whites

The Biracial Brigade and the Mixed Melanin Squad are formally invited as liaisons in the matter of "Spicy/Seasoned Whites — Level 1 Spice Privileges", which remains under Auntie Army review and is filed separately as an open matter.

Note of Record. Tomfoolery is, by operation of this accord, in full effect for all of these perpetrating asshats.
