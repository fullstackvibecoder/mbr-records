The Whimsical-Skedaddling Decree

Imposing one (1) year of forfeit of whimsical skedaddling upon the Caucasian Caucus, as repentance for Raisin Gate.

Whereas the events of Raisin Gate (March 9 – March 18, 2026) brought the Caucasian Caucus into a state of repentance with the chamber;

Whereas the Irish Association, having long custody of certain whimsical Caucasian behaviors, has elected to impose a measured penalty;

Whereas the Caucasian Caucus has accepted the penalty in good faith and without further appeal;

Now, therefore, the Irish Association decrees:

Article I — Forfeit

The Caucasian Caucus shall, for a period of one (1) calendar year commencing March 17, 2026, forfeit the practice of whimsical skedaddling. The forfeit covers:

  1. All forms of skedaddling characterized by undue whimsy;
  2. The use of the verb "skedaddle" in any whimsical sense by any member of the Caucasian Caucus;
  3. Any musical or theatrical accompaniment that would convert ordinary skedaddling into whimsical.

Article II — Permitted Skedaddling

Notwithstanding Article I, the Caucasian Caucus retains the right to plain skedaddling, undertaken with seriousness and without flourish. The Bureau shall, in case of dispute, refer the matter to the Irish Association for determination.

Article III — Term and Expiry

This decree expires automatically on March 17, 2027, at which point whimsical skedaddling shall return to the Caucasian Caucus without further proceedings, provided no further conduct has prompted extension.

Note of Record. This decree is filed separately from the Habibi-for-Veto Accord (MBR-ACC-2026-001), and does not form part of that accord's terms.
